COUPE CARS

A coupe car is a stylish, two-door vehicle designed with a sleek, sloping roofline that gives it a sporty and premium appearance. Coupes prioritize performance, sharper handling, and bold design over practicality, making them ideal for people who enjoy a dynamic driving experience. They usually offer seating for two to four passengers, with compact rear seats and a more driver-focused cabin.

SPORTS CARS

A sports car is a high-performance vehicle built for speed, agility, and an exciting driving experience. It usually features a powerful engine, low-slung body, and sharp handling, allowing it to accelerate quickly and corner with precision. Sports cars often come in 2-door coupe or convertible designs, emphasizing style and aerodynamics. They focus on performance more than practicality, so rear seats (if present) are small and boot space is limited.

SEDAN CARS

A sedan is a comfortable, practical, and spacious car designed with a classic three-box layout—the engine at the front, the cabin in the middle, and a separate enclosed boot at the rear. Sedans usually come with four doors and seating for five passengers, making them ideal for families, daily commuting, and long-distance travel. They offer a smooth ride, good fuel efficiency, and a balanced driving experience. Sedans also tend to have better aerodynamics and a more premium feel compared to hatchbacks or compact cars.

WAGON CARS

A wagon (also called an estate car) is a type of vehicle that combines the comfort of a sedan with the cargo space of an SUV. It has a long roofline and an extended rear section, giving it a large, practical boot area that’s easily accessible through a rear hatch door. Wagons are known for their smooth ride, good fuel efficiency, and car-like handling, while still offering more luggage space than most sedans. They are popular in Europe and among families or travellers who need extra room without moving to a big SUV.

SUVs CARS

An SUV (Sports Utility Vehicle) is a larger, more versatile type of car designed to offer higher ground clearance, powerful performance, and the ability to handle rough roads or off-road conditions. SUVs usually come with a tall body, spacious interiors, and a commanding driving position that gives excellent road visibility. Many SUVs also offer 4×4 or AWD systems for better traction on mud, sand, hills, and uneven terrain, making them suitable for both city and adventure driving. They often provide more seating capacity (5- to 7-seaters), bigger boot space, and better comfort for long trips.
